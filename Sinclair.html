Heres some super Fun Facts about the Earth!

  • Earth's Shape: While often depicted as a sphere, Earth is actually an oblate spheroid, meaning it's slightly flattened at the poles and bulges at the equator.
  • Earth's Rotation: Earth spins on its axis, completing one rotation in approximately 24 hours.
  • Earth's Orbit: Earth orbits the Sun, completing one orbit in roughly 365 days.
  • Earth's Tilt: Earth's tilt (23.4 degrees) is responsible for the seasons.
  • Earth's Interior: Earth's interior is not solid, and its core is mainly made of iron and nickel.
  • Earth's Surface: Water covers over 70% of Earth's surface, and the oceans are the largest bodies of water.
  • Earth's Largest Structure: Coral reefs are the largest living structure on Earth.
  • Earth's Atmosphere: Earth's atmosphere is primarily composed of nitrogen and oxygen.
  • Earth's Moon: Earth has one natural satellite, the Moon, which is drifting away from Earth.
  • Earth's Magnetic Field: Earth has a magnetic field that protects it from harmful solar radiation.
